Abstract

Along my path of a Taijiquan practitioner I cam along many, many fascinating details about this amazing martial art. This is an attempt to collect and persist some of it. For my own purpose and also to share with you.

Content

  • Forms - The core of Taijiquan practice are its solo forms, a sequence of movements, something like Japanese Katas yet different.

  • History - Understanding the past of Taijiquan helps to understand why it is where it is of today.

  • Theory - Half practice, half theory, especially as Taijiquan is a form of applied philosophy, it is crucial to have a deeper understanding of it’s theoretical foundation.

  • Styles - Different family traditions have led to different formal schools with different emphasize.

  • Resources - Some recommendable books, websites, videos; partly used as sources for this wiki.
